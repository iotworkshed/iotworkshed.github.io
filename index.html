Talking Teddy Bear Gift – Part 1

Are you thinking of a gift for that special someone in your life? Here’s an idea, why not make him or her a Talking Teddy Bear! Yes, you can make your Teddy Bear talk with Alexa running on Raspberry Pi! Read on to see how ðŸ™‚

First, look for a cute Teddy that’s perfect for a gift. Next, using a pair of scissors, an old sewing kit and a zipper (tip: you can find a zipper from an old bag laying around), cut the back of the Teddy Bear and sew the zipper. This is where we will insert the microphone to talk to the bear. Once done, it will look something like this:

Then, buy a small backpack or a small pouch that can fit nicely on the back of the bear. In the later part of this blog series, we will install the Raspberry Pi and Speakers inside this small bag.

Once you have a small bag that fits behind the bear, it is time to install Alexa into the Rasperry Pi. Do note that we will need to cut a whole on the bag to create a vent that will allow air to flow into the bag and keep our Raspberry Pi cool and to avoid it from overheating.
